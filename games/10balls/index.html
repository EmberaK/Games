Rules for game:

  • Click the "Play" button to start the game.
  • You and your opponent (the computer) both start with 10 balls.
  • Enter the number of balls you want to bet and click "GO".
  • Choose whether the number will be even or odd by clicking the "Even" or "Odd" button.
  • The computer will also make its guesses and bets.
  • If you guess correctly, you will gain more balls. If not, you will lose your balls.
  • The game continues until one player reaches 20 balls or more.
