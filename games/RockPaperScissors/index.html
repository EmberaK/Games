Rules for game:

  • Two players play: user and computer.
  • To start the game, press the "play" button.
  • Each player chooses rock, paper, or scissors.
  • Rock beats scissors.
  • Scissors beat paper.
  • Paper beats rock.
  • In case of a tie, the game continues until one player reaches 5 points.
